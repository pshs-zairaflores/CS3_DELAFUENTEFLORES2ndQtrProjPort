Aang

Nation: Air Nomads

Personality: A playful, optimistic, and peace-loving, yet deeply responsible and courageous 12-year-old boy.

Character Arc

At the start of the show, Aang is shown to be a teenage boy still struggling with dealing with the fact that he’s the last of his people and is still in denial. His character arc revolves around him balancing his life as the Avatar and as a young boy. From Book 1 to Book 3, Aang has learned how to face the world he has now woken up in, 100 years later. His development goes from being a carefree boy who avoids his problems to a strong Avatar who faces his fears.
